Pregnancies, Births and Abortions in the United States: National and State Trends by Age, 1973-2020

This is the online codebook for the historical estimates of pregnancy, birth and abortion rates by age-group and state produced by the Guttmacher Institute. Click any variable for more details, or filter to a selection of variables. The full dataset, along with links to accompanying documentation, is available at https://osf.io/kthnf/
